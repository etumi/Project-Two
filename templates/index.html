US Gas Pollution

Welcome to our website! With the pollution rates on the rise we decided to examine the growth of the 5 Pollution causing gasses (NO2, O3, SO2, O3 and CO2) from 2000 to 2016 in US. Further, we broke down this data by making meaning full graphs that helped us analyze the trends in the rise of these gasses and which states have the highest rates of these gasses.
